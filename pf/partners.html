"In the Center for Community Engaged Learning, we work to build bridges between the campus community and the rich and vibrant neighborhoods and communities of New York City. Family support for CCEL has made the Center what it is today. Over the past three years, we have been able to double our courses, research projects and community programs thanks to the generous support and thought partners of Fordham families. This support gives our students valuable learning opportunities while also uplifting New Yorkers in need and advancing powerful community projects like our Highbridge Farmer's Market, which brings fresh produce to a food desert in the Bronx. We love our Fordham families!"

Julie L Gafney, PhD

Assistant Vice President, Strategic Mission Initiatives

Executive Director, The Center for Community Engaged Learning
