Parents' Leadership Council members seek to deepen their connection to the Fordham community and other parents through philanthropy and involvement, making an immediate and long-term impact on the student experience

Members of the council:

  • Lead

    Partner with the Career Center to mentor and help indentify career opportunities for students and help indentify career opportunities for students and graduates, from internship positions to employment.

    Leverage corporate and personal relationships to enhance opportunities for students.

  • Serve

    Share the University's mission and values while encouraging other families to join the council and championing Fordham in your local community.

    Attend or host Fordham receptions, dinners, lectures, and other gatherings in your community and on campus.

  • Give

    Make a minimum annual gift of $5000 and consider a major contribution during your tenure on the council.

    Attend at least one or two council gatherings per academic year, where you will get the opportunity to engage with senior leadership on important topics affecting students.

This volunteer group is made up of parents and guardians who are dedicated to enhancing the experience of Fordham students and their families and to furthering the University's mission while forging their own connections and network of peers and friends within the Ramily.

Members of the committee:

  • Provide

    Career mentorship and job opportunities through the Rams Helping Rams Program

  • Attend

    Attend or host presidential and regional chapter events

  • Assist

    In reaching philanthropic goals through peer-to-peer fundraising efforts among members of the Fordham Family Community

  • Volunteer

    To send welcome emails and mak phone calls to new members of the Ramily

  • Help

    To steward fellow parent and family donors with thank-you notes after Fordham Giving Day
