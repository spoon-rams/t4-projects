By virtue of a $5 million gift from Fordham trustee Kim Bepler, whose giving continues to have a deep and wide-ranging impact across the University, Fordham University will establish an endowed chair in the natural and applied sciences, which will be titled the Kim B. and Stephen E. Bepler Chair in the Natural and Applied Sciences. This new position is expected to advance the University’s vision for excellence in science education by fueling new interdisciplinary research into today’s most pressing scientific challenges, and is in addition to four other endowed chair positions in the sciences that she and the estate of her late husband, Steve Bepler, FCRH ’64, funded in 2017.

Dennis Marks Photo

A generous bequest from Drs. Dennis (FCRH ’66) and Patricia Marks will establish a distinguished chair in cosmology within the Physics department at Fordham. Strong supporters of the liberal arts (and sciences!), they believe that the Drs. Dennis and Patricia Marks Chair in Cosmology will give students an opportunity to “tackle big questions” that touch on “the origins, structure, and nature of the universe.” Arts and Sciences is deeply honored to be able to celebrate the Marks’ deep roots at Fordham. In addition to his major in physics from FCRH, Dr. Dennis Marks graduated from the Fordham Preparatory School. And his father, Dr. Louis Marks, was a professor in Fordham’s Biological Sciences department.

James C. McGroddy Photo

The Faculty of Arts and Sciences also received a generous donation from James C. McGroddy to establish a new award that supports leadership and innovation in pedagogy. This new prize will honor tenure-line faculty members whose pedagogical approach has had a significant positive impact on the core curriculum, the development of student leaders, and/or instruction in STEM (i.e., science, technology, engineering, mathematics) or interdisciplinary fields. Recipients will receive $10,000 (divided evenly among the recipients if the award is given to a team). With deep gratitude to James C. McGroddy, we look forward to announcing the winner (s) on Friday, April 28, 2023.
